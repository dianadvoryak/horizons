THE SKIN BENEFITS OF TURMERIC

There’s a saying that goes, “what’s old is new again,” and in the beauty world we think it has some merit. New ingredients and technology are the lifeblood of product innovation, but sometimes it pays to look to the past (in some cases even the ancient past) to inform new skin solutions. We know the value of combining old and new to create something even more effective. One part of the past we constantly draw inspiration from is Ayurveda. Not familiar with Ayurvedic healing? Hailing from Southeast Asia, Ayurveda is a whole-health holistic approach to medicine. It centers around the idea that natural therapies can help regain balance in the mind, body, and spirit to positively impact one’s health. While there are many herbs and plants that are common in Ayurvedic practices, one of the most common is Turmeric. This bright yellow root is wonderful for the skin when incorporated into products, particularly those designed for fighting dullness and the appearance of pigmentation. Which is why we wanted to create the best Turmeric infused skincare products, to bring this ancient plant into the mainstream with efficacious formulations that would create a visible improvement for your complexion. Intrigued? Read on for this informative overview on all things Turmeric and skin.
What is Turmeric? Turmeric is a flowering plant that is part of the same family as ginger. The root of the plant is dried and then crushed up into a powder with a bright yellow hue that is unmistakable. You can usually find it in the spice aisle and it is commonly used in dishes like curry. Turmeric’s active ingredient is called circumin and has anti-inflammatory benefits when incorporated into a healthy diet. It has also been shown to provide antioxidant protection as well.
