Take Your Vitamins

You already know that vitamins are an essential part of a healthy diet. As it turns out, they’re crucial for healthy-looking skin, too. Vitamins—many of which are antioxidants—help neutralize skin-damaging elements in the air called free radicals. Scientifically, free radicals are known as reactive oxygen species. They result from various environmental stressors, like pollution and UV radiation, and alter the molecules that make up your skin barrier. Over time, exposure to free radicals can lead to fine lines, wrinkles, and other signs of premature aging.I One of the most useful (and popular) vitamins in skincare is vitamin C. In addition to helping to neutralize free radicals, vitamin C stimulates collagen production, a protein responsible for giving skin its elasticity.

This can help give skin a firmer, more radiant appearance. It’s also known to inhibit melanin production, which makes vitamin C particularly useful in treating dark spots and other discolorations.
