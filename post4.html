HEALTHY SKIN FOR ALL

We believe in Healthy Skin for All. We encourage inclusivity and celebrate diversity amongst our customers, community and employees. We believe in embracing who we are as individuals of every race, gender, religion, ability and disability, or who we choose to love. Together, we are working toward a brighter future. We’re proud of how far we’ve come – yet acknowledge there is more work to be done. This Pride Month, we come together as a community to celebrate our accomplishments and each other. Together, We Pride On.

For nearly 20 years, Kiehl’s has been a proud supporter of the LGBTQ+ community, as a member of the New York City March since 2002 and becoming an official sponsor of NYC Pride March in 2010. Although Pride looks a little different this year, we are just as proud to continue celebrating and showing our support.

Healthy Skin For All not only means celebrating our diverse and inclusive community but also honoring our unique skincare goals. The journey to healthy skin is different for all of us but we know skincare is self-care. And the right products not only can help you reach your skincare goals but also help you feel more confident in your own skin. We’ve pulled together some daily skincare routines to help your reach your goals and unlock that confidence to be proud of who you are.
