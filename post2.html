Exfoliate To Boost Brightness

We’re constantly shedding dead skin cells and making new ones. But as we age, that process slows, and a buildup of dead cells on your skin’s surface can lead to a tired or dull-looking complexion. Exfoliation can help to remove this buildup of dead cells and promote a smoother, more radiant-looking complexion.

There are two types of exfoliation, chemical and physical, and both can help brighten the skin. However, for immediate results, we recommend reaching for a mild face scrub that won’t strip the skin, such as Clearly Corrective Brightening & Exfoliating Cleanser. This effective cleanser features volcanic ash-derived pearlstone to remove dead skin cells and visibly brighten the complexion. The mild formula is suitable for all skin types and gentle enough to be used morning and night for smoother, more radiant-looking skin.
