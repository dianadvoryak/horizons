Take Your Vitamins
You already know that vitamins are an essential part of a healthy diet. As it turns out, they’re crucial for healthy-looking skin, too. Vitamins—many of which are antioxidants—help neutralize skin-damaging elements in the air called free radicals. Scientifically, free radicals are known as reactive oxygen species...

Exfoliate To Boost Brightness
We’re constantly shedding dead skin cells and making new ones. But as we age, that process slows, and a buildup of dead cells on your skin’s surface can lead to a tired or dull-looking complexion. Exfoliation can help to remove this buildup of dead cells and promote a smoother, more radiant-looking complexion...
